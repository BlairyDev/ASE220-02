Racy, a European Hedgehog, has a peculiar love for toy cars. His owner discovered this when Racy started pushing a small, remote-controlled car around with his nose. Now, Racy is often seen happily navigating the house in his little car, spinning in circles and even "racing" down hallways. His love for movement and exploration is typical of hedgehogs, but his affinity for driving makes him a local legend among his owner's friends.
